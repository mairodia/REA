Welcome to the Real Estate Association

Where future professionals thrive


Meetings every Thursday during U-Hour (12 - 1PM) in building 163-2020


The vision of the Cal Poly Real Estate Association is to serve as an active, well respected hub of students, faculty, alumni, and professionals who are committed to furthering the quality of real estate education. The organization creates learning and networking opportunities that help students prepare for careers in the real estate industry and provides a strong business network for industry participants.


This year...

For the year of 2017-2018, we have accomplished much, and hope to accomplish more. Among the things that we have done or planned to do are:

  • Brought the Argus Software Program to students
  • Have had over a dozen well-established professionals as guest speakers and mentors for our program
  • Acquired 23 new members since last year
  • Planned field trips to the headquarters of leading real estate businesses such as CBRE and the Irvine Company
  • Planned to bring students on tours to development sites
  • Students that participated in REA have had a higher rate of employment within the real estate industry.
